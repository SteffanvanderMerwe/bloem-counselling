Services

Personalised counselling for every stage of life

Individual

Emotional & Inner Healing

Embark on a journey of self-discovery and growth in a confidential, warm and safe space. Reserved just for you: time to explore your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. Whether you are facing challenges such as anxiety, depression, relationship issues, or simply seeking personal development, I will support you every step of the way.

Healing

Grief Counselling

Navigating the journey of grief can feel overwhelming and isolating, but you don't have to go through it alone. I render compassionate grief counselling to support you through the process of healing and finding hope after loss.

Whether you are struggling with overwhelming stages of grief, restlessness, guilt of moving on, or complicated grief — I'm here to listen, validate your feelings, and help you navigate the complexities of grief.

All Ages

Supporting Adults, Teens & Children

Life's challenges can affect individuals of all ages. I offer compassionate counselling services tailored to support adults, teens, and children through every stage of life's journey — including anxiety, depression, school-related stress, self-regulation, and more.

I apply age-appropriate techniques and play-based counselling to help children express themselves, process difficult emotions, and develop healthy coping strategies in a supportive and nurturing environment.

Wellness

Temperament, Stress & Anxiety Counselling

Life's challenges can be overwhelming, and it's alright to seek assistance navigating through them. I provide a safe and supportive environment where you can explore the root causes of your stress and anxiety and develop personalised coping mechanisms.

Prolonged stress and anxiety is stored in our bodies and presents as headaches, clenched muscles, tension in your shoulders, heart palpitations, tight chest, digestive issues, immune suppression, and more. Together, let's embark on a journey towards healing and greater well-being.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness & Self Regulation

I am a firm believer that true success begins from within. I'm dedicated to promoting mindfulness and self-regulation counselling techniques that empower individuals to navigate life's challenges with clarity and resilience.

Mindfulness is the practice of being fully present and engaged in the present moment, without judgment or attachment to thoughts, feelings or emotions. Benefits include stress reduction, improved focus, better peace of mind, enhanced emotional regulation, and increased self-awareness.

About Me

Getting to know your counsellor

Established 2023

A Passion for
Guiding People

Bloem Counselling was established in 2023, building on five years of experience as a Social Worker. My passion for counselling and guiding human beings through their challenges has led me to this fulfilling profession.

My goal is to assist and make a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals, couples and families. I facilitate and guide positive change from all stages of life.

My enthusiasm for counselling is deeply rooted in a commitment to support, empower, and foster positive transformation.

I obtained my Bachelor of Social Work degree at the University of South Africa whilst working part time.
